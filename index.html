Riskshark

Riskshark is a product used by analysts which makes it easier for them to analyze the risk status of a company. The purpose of this product is to keep the user updated with all the filings and provide the following analysis:

  • Risk score
  • Differences between the risk factors’ section of consecutive filings of the same type
  • Sentiment Analysis - Count of certain words in different categories

  • Keep track of bookings (In-progress, completed, cancelled, no-show)
  • Assign bookings efficiently to crews based on shifts
  • Allow dispatchers to manage vehicles, crews and bookings
GRT Health

The GRTHealth Community Care platform is a one-stop Virtual Care Centre focusing on enabling the elderly or those previously requiring hospitalized long-term care, to remain independent in the home. This application will collect & display non-medical monitoring data from the devices installed in seniors’ homes. The devices will be registered on Sigfox and will use the Sigfox network to transmit the data to the application server. The purpose of non-medical monitoring is to ensure the safety of Patients in the home.

Carehubble

CareHubble is an online platform where Clients may search and schedule qualified home care Providers. The platform enables secure communication & transactions between Clients and Providers, compliant storage of Client health & payment information, and Provider account deposit information. Visit logs and electronic check-in and check-out give Clients peace of mind and confidence in the accountabilities of Providers. Providers set their own hourly rates and manage their Clients and schedules autonomously.

Bloomly

A Bloomly NFT marketplace is a platform that allows users to create and launch their own branded NFT marketplaces. This means that the platform provides the underlying technology and infrastructure for the marketplace, while the user can customize the branding and user experience to suit their own needs. This can be a useful solution for individuals or organizations that want to create their own NFT marketplace without having to build the technology from scratch.
